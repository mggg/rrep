Autauga County Alabama Board of Education Election (November 8, 2022) RPV-Ready File

## RDH Date Retrieval
06/24/24

## Sources
Alabama Secretary of State, VEST, Autauga County Probate Office, L2 and U.S. Census Bureau

This file contains the following datasets aggregated to the precinct-level:

- Election results, from the Alabama Secretary of State (https://www.sos.alabama.gov/alabama-votes/voter/election-data)

- Precinct boundaries, from the RDH file with data from VEST 2020 Shapefile and Autauga County Probate Office, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/alabama-2022-general-election-precinct-level-results-and-boundaries/)

- 2020 PL data, from the US Census Bureau, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/alabama-block-pl-94171-2020/)

- 2022 CVAP data, from the US Census Bureau, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/alabama-block-cvap-data-2022/)

- Election turnout data from L2 via RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/2022-alabama-l2-voter-file-elections-turnout-statistics-aggregated-to-2020-census-blocks/)

For the election results, absentee and early votes were assigned to precincts based on that precincts share of election-day voting.

Census blocks were assigned to precincts based on the precinct that contained the majority of a census block's area.

Additional election results are available in the precinct boundaries and election results file on the RDH website, linked above.

PL field descriptions available on the RDH website: https://redistrictingdatahub.org/data/about-our-data/pl-94171-dataset/fields-and-descriptions/
