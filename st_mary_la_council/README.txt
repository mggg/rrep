St. Mary Parish Louisana Parish Council Election (October 14, 2023) RPV-Ready File

## RDH Date Retrieval
06/24/24

## Sources
Louisiana Secretary of State, Louisiana State Legislature, U.S. Census Bureau

This file contains the following datasets aggregated to the precinct-level:

- Election results, from the Louisiana Secretary of State (https://www.sos.la.gov/ElectionsAndVoting/GetElectionInformation/FindResultsAndStatistics/Pages/default.aspx)

- Precinct boundaries, from the Louisiana State Legislature, via the RDH website, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/louisiana-2023-primary-and-general-election-precinct-level-results/)

- 2020 PL data, from the US Census Bureau, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/louisiana-block-pl-94171-2020/)

- 2022 CVAP data, from the US Census Bureau, via the RDH website (https://redistrictingdatahub.org/dataset/louisiana-block-cvap-data-2022/)

- Election turnout data from Louisiana Secretary of State (https://www.sos.la.gov/ElectionsAndVoting/Pages/PostElectionStatisticsStatewide.aspx)

For the election results, absentee and early votes were assigned to precincts based on that precincts share of election-day voting.

Census blocks were assigned to precincts based on the precinct that contained the majority of a census block's area.

Additional election results are available in the precinct boundaries and election results file on the RDH website, linked above.

PL field descriptions available on the RDH website: https://redistrictingdatahub.org/data/about-our-data/pl-94171-dataset/fields-and-descriptions/
